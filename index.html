Who we are

Making Disciples of all Nations

The New Covenant Church started in October 1985 with the first worship venue at the Women Training Centre, Samonda, in Ibadan, Nigeria. In 1986, the first centre in the United Kingdom started at Elephant and Castle, London. We presently have over 500 branches of the Church in Africa, Europe, America, Middle East and Asia. Over 400 of the branches are located in Nigeria

MISSION STATEMENT

To bring every member into maturity in Christ. To foster deep and meaningful fellowship. To encourage and work for unity among all believers. To work for the good of the Community.

DISCIPLESHIP

In the Great Commission, our Lord commands us to “make disciples of all nations”. To realistically evangelize the world every believer must be equipped and empowered to win souls and bring them to maturity.

BIBLICAL

A return to the original pattern of the church entails engaging only in biblical teaching and practices, especially as expounded in the New Testament by Jesus Christ Himself in the Gospels, and by the Apostles in the Epistles. If Jesus is the Founder and Head of the Church, then the Church needs to preach, teach and practice only what He (Jesus) taught.

One Church

Every member of New Covenant Church,regardless of race, ethnicity, social status or nationality, belongs to one big loving and caring family where agape love is not only preached but practised effectively. Moreover, every member of your biological family can find fulfillment in our Children/Teenage Church; Youth Church or Adult Church.

We are a happy church, and our happiness is derived from our understanding that Jesus Christ’s finished work on the cross provides for our all-round prosperity: in our work, health, family and relationships. Our programmes are deliberately targeted at making disciples: getting people to know Jesus Christ; being Christ-like; and joyfully serving Him, as we provide platforms for the fulfillment of individual ministries.
